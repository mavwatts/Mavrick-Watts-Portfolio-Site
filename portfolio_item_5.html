Microbrew For You Site

OVERVIEW

Microbrew for you is a social site for beer enthusiast and connoisseurs alike. It combines the best of social media and your favorite beer all into one. Searching for your favorite beer? Need a new spot to drink? Want to meet people interested in the same brews as you? This is the site to make the bond.

CHALLENGES

For this Python based website I worked in a team of four in a SCRUM environment. Challenges that we faced as were coming up with a working function to loop through all of the different brew types, a suitable UI, and an API for the different breweries.

We opted for a simple card based approach to separate the different models and their functional concerns.


THE SOLUTION

My team and I came up with a plan to create a site that functions similar to a social media site where you can follow friends, show your profile picture, have brewery location suggestions, and save your favorite breweries all on your own page. From the beginning we established that there would be a sign up form just like a social media site to ensure users are able to follow only what beers they want, there post history, and friends.

We worked in an SCRUM setting and tasked individuals with tickets along with daily merges. My task for this site was working on the functionality of the favorite brew types, the post functionality and contributing the backend models and forms
