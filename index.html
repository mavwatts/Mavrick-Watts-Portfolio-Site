Hi, I'm Mavrick. Im a multi-disciplinary maker with over 4 years of experiences in a range of design disciplines.


Over the years I've held many titles such as videographer, photographer, web designer, content specialist, but overall I would identify myself as a creator. There is no master level for me as I prefer to keep learning, challenging myself, and do interesting things that matter. This frame of mind keeps me humble, growing, and ever learning.

My abundant energy fuels me in the pursuit of many creative interests, hobbies, and artistic endeavors. Im a quick learner, able to pick up new skills, and juggle different projects and roles. I developed this mindset as I would sometimes not have a team available to help me with projects so I would take on the challenge on myself.

One passion that I've always resonated with has been web design, particularly front end web design. I've always been in the tech field but being able to marry technology with my artistic side is why I enjoy this field. While it may be difficult I don't consider it to be work as much as a unique puzzle to solve. Each website is unique which appeals to the inner artist in me as each piece is different. From creating simple landing pages to sites for business's to establish a web presence I truly enjoy this craft.
