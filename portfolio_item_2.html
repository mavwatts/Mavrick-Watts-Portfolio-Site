Mavrick Cinematics Page

Founded in at the beginning of 2019, Mavrick Cinematics is a media production company focused on creating the most visually appealing and compelling video content to tell your story. Mavrick Cinematics offers videography, video editing, and drone videography services.

OVERVIEW

As a growing media production company one crucial challenge when beginning to scale is how to best display a strong body of work to its users effectively.

For this project I set out with the goal of making a single page web app (SPA) that displays Mavrick Cinematic's videography work to land more clients. I opted for a landing page that incorporated a video background to immediately let visitors know this is video website. Other goals I had were to utilize javascript in some sort of fashion as well as make the site mobile friendly for users.

CHALLENGES

Challenges that I had with this project were the side hamburger menu which I got through several rounds of trial and error. I originally had the site as multiple pages but after research I found the SPA's are better received by users and adjusted the menu to just smoothly travel to the desired section.

THE SOLUTION

The solution I came up with for displaying portfolio work was to highlight the best body of work while using a button to guide users to the facebook page where they could follow and see more sample work.
